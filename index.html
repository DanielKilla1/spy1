Trading Strategy Rules

Entry Conditions

  • The first 1-hour candle of the trading day must have a range of at least the selected range requirement
  • Long trade → if the first 1-hour candle closes above the previous day's close
  • Short trade → if the first 1-hour candle closes below the previous day's close
  • No trade is taken if the first candle closes exactly equal to the previous day's close

Trade entry occurs at the close of the first 1-hour candle. Maximum of one trade per day.

Exit Conditions

Two exit strategies are available:

  • ATR-Based Stop Loss: Dynamic stop loss based on market volatility using Average True Range
    • Customizable ATR multiplier to adjust stop loss distance
    • Adaptable to market conditions - wider in high volatility, tighter in low volatility
  • Default Strategy: Fixed stop loss based on first candle's range
    • For Long Positions: Exit if any following 1-hour candle breaks below the first 1-hour candle's low
    • For Short Positions: Exit if any following 1-hour candle breaks above the first 1-hour candle's high
  • Monthly Profit Protection: Prevents losing more than a percentage of monthly profits
    • Tracks profits on a monthly basis
    • Stops taking new trades once monthly loss limit is reached
    • Ensures you preserve a portion of each month's gains
  • Risk-Reward Filter: Only takes trades with favorable risk-to-reward ratios
    • Calculates potential risk based on stop distance
    • Ensures reward potential justifies the risk taken
  • Profit Target: Exit when price reaches the selected profit target (configurable from 0.1 to 30 points)
  • End of Day: If neither stop loss nor profit target is hit, exit at the last 1-hour candle's close

Trade Management

  • Position Size: $10,000 per trade (not adjusted for price or leverage)
  • Maximum Trades per Day: 1
  • No compounding or reinvestment of gains
